The data were extracted from the ClimDB/HydroDB database with SQL statements and converted into tables conforming to Observations Data Model (ODM) 1.1. Export and EML creation scripts can be found at https://github.com/lter/Clim-HydroDB-2.0. Appropriate text metadata (not available from ClimDB/HydroDB) was added, including abstract, these extraction methods, keywords, and geographic coverage (if missing from ClimDB/HydroDB).
